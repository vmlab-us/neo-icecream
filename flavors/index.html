FLAVORS

Current available flavor visuals

This page uses only the currently available real flavor files. No invented products or unsupported formats are shown.

Vanilla

Vanilla is the cleanest and most familiar flavor direction in the NEOICECREAM line. It helps present the product as an easy-to-understand frozen dessert bar with a smooth, classic profile and broad consumer appeal.

Mango

Mango brings a bright fruit-forward direction to the NEOICECREAM frozen dessert bar concept. It adds a tropical, refreshing profile while keeping the product aligned with lighter indulgence and modern frozen dessert expectations.

Pistachio

Pistachio gives NEOICECREAM a more premium and distinctive flavor direction. It connects the frozen dessert bar concept with a richer, nut-inspired profile while staying within the brand’s modern better-for-you positioning.

Raspberry

Raspberry adds a sharp, fruit-led flavor direction with a clean and recognizable profile. It helps balance the NEOICECREAM line with a refreshing berry option for consumers looking for a lighter frozen dessert experience.

Chocolate

Chocolate provides a familiar indulgent direction within the NEOICECREAM frozen dessert bar line. It supports the idea that a better-for-you frozen dessert can still feel classic, satisfying, and dessert-driven.

Strawberry

Strawberry gives NEOICECREAM a familiar fruit flavor with broad consumer appeal. It supports the brand’s lighter frozen dessert direction while keeping the product approachable, recognizable, and easy to enjoy.
