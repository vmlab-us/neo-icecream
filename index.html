A Better-for-You Frozen Dessert Bar Concept

NEOICECREAM is being developed as a better-for-you frozen dessert bar concept for modern consumers who want a lighter way to enjoy frozen treats. The product direction focuses on low-fat frozen dessert formulation, lower-calorie frozen dessert indulgence, live cultures, and synbiotic-inspired formulation logic, while keeping taste, texture, and everyday enjoyment at the center.

Explore the product concept or see flavor directions.

Frozen Dessert First

NEOICECREAM is not positioned for medical or therapeutic use. It is a frozen dessert first - created to deliver a familiar, enjoyable ice cream alternative with a more thoughtful formulation direction.
