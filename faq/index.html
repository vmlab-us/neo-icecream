FAQ

Common questions

Clear answers about NEOICECREAM, its frozen dessert category, lower-fat profile, real product visuals, and business inquiries.

Is NEOICECREAM ice cream?
No. Traditional ice cream in the U.S. generally requires a higher milkfat level, while NEOICECREAM is designed as a frozen dessert with a low-fat formulation direction. The idea is to give consumers familiar taste, texture, and enjoyment in a lighter, lower-calorie direction.
What is NEOICECREAM?
NEOICECREAM is a modern frozen dessert bar concept created for people who want familiar dessert pleasure in a lighter format. The product is built around real dessert taste, creamy texture, live cultures, and a synbiotic-inspired formulation layer.
What is the main real format shown on the site?
The main format currently shown is the bar. It is portioned, convenient, easy to understand, and supports the product’s better-for-you positioning by giving consumers a controlled dessert format.
Are all of the products shown real current visuals?
Yes. The site uses the available real product visuals only. This keeps the presentation honest and focused on the actual product direction, without showing invented formats or unsupported product images.
Does the site make medical or therapeutic claims?
No. NEOICECREAM is positioned as a better-for-you frozen dessert, not as a medical or therapeutic product. The key consumer message is familiar dessert enjoyment with a lighter, lower-fat profile.
Can investors or partners get more information?
Yes. The public website keeps the message simple and consumer-facing. More detailed information about the product, manufacturing strategy, and business opportunity can be shared upon request with relevant investors or strategic partners.
What makes NEOICECREAM different from traditional ice cream?
NEOICECREAM is being developed as a better-for-you frozen dessert bar concept, not as a traditional high-fat ice cream. The product direction focuses on lighter indulgence, low-fat formulation, lower-calorie positioning, live cultures, and a synbiotic-inspired formulation layer.
Is NEOICECREAM a low-fat frozen dessert?
Yes, the product direction is focused on a low-fat frozen dessert profile. NEOICECREAM is designed around lighter indulgence while still keeping taste, texture, and dessert enjoyment at the center.
Is NEOICECREAM lower in calories?
NEOICECREAM is being developed with a lower-calorie direction compared with many traditional frozen dessert products. Final nutrition information will depend on the completed formulation and commercial production process.
What does “better-for-you frozen dessert” mean?
For NEOICECREAM, better-for-you means a frozen dessert concept with a more thoughtful formulation direction: lower fat, lower-calorie positioning, live cultures, and a modern bar format. It does not mean the product is a medical or therapeutic food.
What does “synbiotic-inspired” mean?
Synbiotic-inspired means the product concept is influenced by the combination of live cultures and food science logic. NEOICECREAM uses this as a supporting formulation direction, without making medical or therapeutic claims.
Does NEOICECREAM contain live cultures?
NEOICECREAM is being developed with live cultures as part of its formulation direction. Final product details, including labeling and any quantitative information, will depend on final validation, production, and regulatory review.
Why does NEOICECREAM focus on bars first?
Bars are the primary format because they offer convenience, portion control, clear flavor presentation, and strong compatibility with retail freezer environments. The bar format also supports a simple and modern consumer experience.
Are the flavors final?
The current flavor directions include vanilla, mango, pistachio, raspberry, chocolate, and strawberry. Final commercial flavors may be adjusted during formulation, pilot production, and consumer testing.
Is NEOICECREAM available in stores?
NEOICECREAM is currently in development and is not yet available in stores. The brand is preparing for pilot production and future commercial launch planning.
Where will NEOICECREAM launch first?
The initial commercial strategy is focused on the U.S. market, with California as a key launch area. Final launch timing and distribution will depend on production, partnerships, and retail planning.
Can retailers or distributors contact NEOICECREAM?
Yes. Retailers, distributors, and strategic partners can contact NEOICECREAM through the Strategic Conversations page.
Can investors contact NEOICECREAM?
Yes. NEOICECREAM is open to strategic investor conversations related to pilot production, manufacturing, launch planning, and long-term brand development.
