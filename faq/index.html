FAQ

Common questions

Clear answers about NEOICECREAM, its frozen dessert category, lower-fat profile, real product visuals, and business inquiries.

Is NEOICECREAM ice cream?
No — and that is exactly one of the product’s advantages. Traditional ice cream in the U.S. generally requires at least 10% milkfat, while NEOICECREAM is designed as a frozen dessert with approximately 2.5% fat.

The idea is to give consumers the familiar taste, texture, and enjoyment of ice cream, but in a lighter, lower-fat, lower-calorie format. If the product were made to qualify as traditional ice cream, it would lose the very advantage that makes it different.
What is NEOICECREAM?
NEOICECREAM is a modern frozen dessert created for people who want the familiar pleasure of ice cream, but in a lighter format. The product is built around real dessert taste, creamy texture, and a lower-fat profile.
What is the main real format shown on the site?
The main format currently shown is the bar. It is portioned, convenient, easy to understand, and supports the product’s better-for-you positioning by giving consumers a controlled dessert format.
Are all of the products shown real current visuals?
Yes. The site uses the available real product visuals only. This keeps the presentation honest and focused on the actual product direction, without showing invented formats or unsupported product images.
Does the site make medical or therapeutic claims?
No. NEOICECREAM is positioned as a better-for-you frozen dessert, not as a medical or therapeutic product. The key consumer message is simple: familiar dessert enjoyment with a lighter, lower-fat profile.
Can investors or partners get more information?
Yes. The public website keeps the message simple and consumer-facing. More detailed information about the product, manufacturing strategy, and business opportunity can be shared upon request with relevant investors or strategic partners.
