What is NEOICECREAM?
NEOICECREAM is a modern frozen dessert concept built around real dessert enjoyment, lighter indulgence direction, and a clear primary bar format.
Is it positioned as ice cream in the U.S.?
No. The public-facing U.S. category used on the site is frozen dessert.
What is the main real format shown on the site?
The main real format currently shown is the bar. The site intentionally leads with that format because those are the current real visual assets.
Are all of the products shown real current visuals?
Yes. The current version is limited to the available real product visuals provided for the project.
Does the site make medical or therapeutic claims?
No. The wording is intentionally conservative and avoids medical, disease, or therapeutic claims.
Can investors or partners get more information?
Yes. The site keeps strategic and investor information general, while detailed materials are available upon request.
