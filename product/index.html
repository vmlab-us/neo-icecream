Frozen Dessert Bars Designed for Lighter Indulgence

NEOICECREAM is developing frozen dessert bars for consumers who want a more modern approach to indulgence. The product concept focuses on a low-fat frozen dessert direction, a lower-calorie profile, and a taste-led experience that still feels creamy, satisfying, and easy to understand.

Why Frozen Dessert Bars?

Bars are the primary format for NEOICECREAM because they offer portion control, convenience, retail freezer compatibility, and a simple consumer experience. A frozen dessert bar is easy to hold, easy to serve, and easy to recognize as a modern alternative to traditional ice cream formats.

A Synbiotic-Inspired Formulation Layer

NEOICECREAM uses a synbiotic-inspired formulation direction as a supporting layer of the product concept. This means the brand is inspired by the combination of live cultures and food science logic, without making medical or therapeutic claims.

Taste Comes First

The product is designed as a frozen dessert first. The goal is to create a bar that feels enjoyable, creamy, and familiar, while also reflecting consumer interest in lighter and more thoughtful frozen dessert options.
